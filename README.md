# US Car Accidents Analysis

This project visualizes key patterns in US car accident data to uncover insights that can be used to enhance road safety and encourage positive policies. The Dashboard can be used to assess key metrics and how they correlate with accidents.

- Geographical Distribution
Visualizes accident geographically across US states and regions.
- Frequency & Severity
Highlights accident counts and severity levels to identify high-risk zones.
- Signals, Road Types & Accident Patterns
Analyzes how traffic signals and road infrastructure correlate with accident occurrences.
- Monthly Distribution
Tracks seasonal trends and monthly fluctuations in accident rates.
- Weather Conditions
Assesses the impact of rain, fog, snow, and other weather factors on accident frequency.
- Temperature vs Accidents
Explores how temperature extremes influence accident likelihood.
- Daylight Conditions
Compares accident rates under daylight, and nighttime conditions.
